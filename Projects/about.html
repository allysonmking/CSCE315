About Me

Education

I am a Dallas native but currently call Aggieland home. This is my senior year at Texas A&M (whoop!) where I am pursuing degrees in both statistics and computer science. My dream is to become a data scientist- a role where I can use my curious and analytical nature to problem-solve and provide meaning to the world around me. I will graduate in May 2021 and am excited to see what's next!

Faith

I love the Lord with my whole heart and am thankful for his abounding grace, continual presence, and incredible goodness. His mercy rescues me every day! Being a member of and serving my church is a huge part of my life.

Family

Family means the world to me! Living just a few minutes away from grandparents, aunts, uncles, and cousins my whole life has been a huge blessing. I am incredibly grateful for the care, encouragement, and love my family has endlessly showered me with. Time spent together is sweet and full of laughs (and good food)!

This past summer, a “mom & me” trip to Yellowstone National Park bumped my state count to 35. One day I hope to step foot in all 50!
