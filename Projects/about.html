About Me

Education

I am a Dallas native but currently call Aggieland home. This is my senior year at Texas A&M (whoop!) where I am pursuing degrees in both statistics and computer science. My dream is to become a data scientist- a role where I can use my curious and analytical nature to problem-solve and provide meaning to the world around me. I will graduate in May 2021 and am excited to see what the future will hold!

Family

Family means the world to me! Living just a few minutes away from grandparents, aunts, uncles, and cousins my whole life has been a huge blessing. I am incredibly grateful for the care, encouragement, and love my family has endlessly showered me with. Time spent together is sweet and full of laughs (and good food)!

Faith

“Be strong and courageous; don’t be terrified or afraid of them. For the Lord, your God is the one who will go with you; he will not leave you or abandon you -- Deuteronomy 31:6 (CSB)”. I love the Lord with my whole heart and am thankful for his abounding grace, continual presence, and incredible goodness. His mercy rescues me every day.

Travel

Many of my most treasured memories were made while traveling! Hiking up mountains, strolling along the beach, walking through historical villages – all of these fulfil my love for adventure. This past summer, a “mom & me” trip to Yellowstone National Park bumped my state count to 35. One day I hope to step foot in all 50!

Hobbies

I never run out of ideas for ways to fill my spare time! Competitive sports were a big part of my childhood, so I love playing volleyball, tennis, basketball, and soccer whenever I get a chance. Music is another activity I enjoy as well; I play the piano and am (slowly) learning the guitar.
